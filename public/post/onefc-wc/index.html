ONE Featherweight Kickboxing World GP: Petrosyan vs Petchmorakot

2019-05-20
R
197 words 1 min read

Last Friday, ONE-fc come out with one of the biggest and most anticipated shows in the history of the sport, with 8 among the best kickboxers in the World battling for the Featherweight Kicknboxing Grand Prix belt and the prize of one million dollars! One of the favorites, Giorgio “the Doctor” Petrosyan lost against the 2 division Lumpinee Stadium champion Petchmorakot Pechindee Accademy and, as often happens for split decisions, the result generated some frustation among the fan base. Who landed more stikes and how tight was the fight?. I carefully looked at the video of the fight in slowmotion, patiently scored each single strike using a profession (event logging software)[https://www.boris.unito.it/] and come out with few charts of stats to answer those questions.
